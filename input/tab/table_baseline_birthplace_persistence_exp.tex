\item \footnotesize \textit{Notes:} This table regresses an employment indicator on experienced birthplace FLPF and MLFP, current district fixed effects, and other individual characteristics. The experienced birthplace FLFP (MLFP) corresponds to the district FLFP (MFLP) in the nearest Indonesian census before the year of emigration. Panel A restricts the sample to one-time migrants, while Panel B restricts it to one-time migrants who left their birthplace before they turned 18. The coefficients are normalized so that they can be interpreted as a change in the likelihood of employment from an IQR increase in experienced FLFP.
